Election 2020

Each bar below is sized to represent the number of votes received by Joe Biden (blue) or Donald Trump (red) in the 2020 election. Each state is tinted red or blue based on the percentage of margin of victory in the state. States that are nearly tied appear as white. States that were overwhelmingly won by one candidate or the other will appear slightly blue (for Joe Biden) or red (for Donald Trump). The data is pulled live from the Cook Political Report's 2020 Popular Vote Tracker.


This map was developed by David Gotz at the UNC VACLab. It is a 2020 update to the map discussed in this 2016 blog post about the misleading nature of some types of election maps.
